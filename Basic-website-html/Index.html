Why get a cat in the first place?

Because cats are awesome!
Cats are cute little 4-legged animals who are filled with love and affection.

Taking care of a cat requires that you provide plenty of human companionship, and regular, suitable meals with a constant supply of fresh water. You will also need to a clean and comfortable bed. Provide the cat with outdoor access or be prepared to empty and clean a litter tray on a daily basis.

If you are thinking about getting a cat,check out this page to learn more about how to take care of a cat before you decide to get one.
Taking care of a cat can also rack up your expenses. You'll have to keep a budget in mind for things like:
  • litter
  • cat food
  • vet expenses(insurance)
  • cat toys
